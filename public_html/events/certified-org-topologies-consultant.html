Brussels (C-OTC): Bootcamp – Design AI-Ready Organizations with Org Topologies, 6–7 October 2026

Design AI-Ready Organizations — Powered by Org Topologies™

📅 6–7 October 2026 📍 Brussels, Belgium ⏱ 2 days 🏅 Certification: C-OTC

Turn AI Speed into System-Wide Performance
Through Strategic Org Design

Organisations are racing to adopt AI — but without redesigning how teams are structured, how decisions flow, and how work is coordinated, AI only amplifies existing dysfunction. The result: faster noise, not better outcomes.

This bootcamp gives leaders and consultants the framework, tools, and hands-on practice to design organisations that are structurally ready for AI — using Org Topologies™, the first People + AI management and org change system.

What This Certification Gives You

A diagnostic framework

Map team types, coupling, and flow across the organisation

🧭

A consulting approach

Facilitate design workshops and guide evolutionary change safely

🤖

An AI-ready lens

Apply the framework to People + AI team models and org change

The Brussels (C-OTC) Bootcamp equips leaders and consultants with the depth, fluency, and confidence to use the Org Topologies™ map in real engagements — from diagnosing structural dysfunction to designing AI-ready organisations and guiding teams through the transition.

What You'll Learn

A hands-on 2-day bootcamp that builds real capability to design AI-ready organisations with the Org Topologies™ framework.

01  The Org Topologies™ Map

Develop fluency with the full Org Topologies™ map: team types, topology patterns, and the principles that determine which structures support or hinder flow. Learn to read an organisation's topology before prescribing any change.

02  Diagnosing Organisational Structure

Practice applying the framework to real-world cases: identify structural dysfunctions, surface hidden dependencies, and understand how current topology constrains or enables the organisation's goals and flow of value.

03  AI and People + AI Teams

Explore how AI changes team composition, decision-making, and org design. Learn to apply the Org Topologies™ framework to People + AI models — the first management system designed for AI-augmented organisations.

04  Facilitating Design Conversations

Learn how to run effective topology workshops with leadership teams and practitioners. Develop facilitation skills for navigating resistance, surfacing assumptions, and building shared understanding — without imposing solutions.

05  Guiding Evolutionary Change

Structural change is high-stakes. Explore how to sequence transitions, manage the human side of reorganisation, and support clients in making evolutionary moves rather than disruptive big-bang restructures.
