ABOUT RE-EVENTED

THE STORY

Re-Evented was formed in 2016 as a non-profit by a team who invests not just time and energy, but also heart and resources into every gathering. Organizing is our way of learning—each event is an experiment, a chance to grow, and a celebration of what's possible when playful seriousness meets real purpose. We're driven by intrinsic motivation and guided by an agile mindset: we adapt, we improve, and we have fun doing it.

THE APPROACH

We organize conferences, unconferences, and workshops that transform organizations through authentic community connections, connecting practitioners across methodologies throughout Benelux and internationally. Every event is a learning journey for us, too—we experiment, reflect, and adapt, always with a spirit of playful seriousness.

THE IMPACT

10 years of proven excellence, continuous innovation, and genuine community building. Our non-profit dedication ensures every decision serves knowledge building over commercial interests. We improve with every step, and we have fun along the way.
